About Me

I am currently a student at Ada Developers Academy. Ada's intensive curriculumn is preparing me to become a full stack junior web developer. I am excited to get out in the field and start creating impactful designs. I am also driven to help make the tech industry more diverse and inclusive.

I was born and raised in Chicago. Both of my parents are Mexican and have taught me to embrace my heritage. I am passionate about giving back to my community and helping fellow Latinx folks interested in tech accomplish their dreams.

In my spare time, I am either reading books or practicing calligraphy. I also spend a lot of time practicing yoga and pilates to unwind.
